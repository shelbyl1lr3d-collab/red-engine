# 🎵 Music Distribution Tools - Friendly Use

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Choose Your Distribution Service

| Service | Link | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| **Jumpstr** | https://jumpstr.io | Free, 100% royalties |
| **Be Musix** | https://bemusix.com | Free + VEVO videos |
| **RouteNote** | https://routenote.com | Free, 85% royalties |
| **Backtune** | https://backtune.net | Free, 150+ platforms |

### Step 2: Prepare Your Music

**What you need:**
- Song file (WAV or MP3)
- Cover art (3000x3000 pixels)
- Song title
- Artist name
- Genre
- Release date

### Step 3: Upload

1. Click the link above
2. Create free account
3. Upload your song
4. Add cover art
5. Fill in details
6. Submit!
