# Gmail → ProtonMail Forwarding Guide

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Part 1: Set Up Forwarding in Gmail

1. **Log into Gmail**
   - Go to https://mail.google.com
   - Sign in as `shelbygriessel@gmail.com`

2. **Open Settings**
   - Click the **gear icon** (top right)
   - Click **"See all settings"**

3. **Go to Forwarding Tab**
   - Click **"Forwarding and POP/IMAP"** tab

4. **Add Forwarding Address**
   - Click **"Add a forwarding address"**
   - Enter your ProtonMail address (e.g., `yourname@protonmail.com`)
   - Click **"Next"**
   - Click **"Proceed"**

5. **Verify in ProtonMail**
   - Open your ProtonMail inbox
   - Find the verification email from Google
   - Click the **verification link** in that email

6. **Back in Gmail**
   - Return to Settings → Forwarding tab
   - Select **"Forward a copy of incoming mail to your ProtonMail address"**
   - Choose: **"keep Gmail's copy in the inbox"** (recommended)
   - Click **"Save Changes"**

---

### Part 2: Save Storage Space in Gmail

**Delete Large Emails:**
1. In Gmail search bar, type: `larger:10MB`
2. Select all results
3. Click trash icon

**Empty Trash:**
1. Click **"Trash"** in left sidebar
2. Click **"Empty Trash now"**

**Delete Old Promotions:**
1. Click **"Promotions"** tab
2. Select all
3. Delete

---

### Part 3: Access Gmail from ProtonMail (Optional)

If you want to send emails FROM Gmail using ProtonMail:

1. Download **ProtonMail Bridge** (free)
   - https://proton.me/mail/bridge
2. Install and log in
3. Add your Gmail account in ProtonMail Bridge
4. Now you can access Gmail from the ProtonMail app

---

### Important Notes
- Forwarding is **instant** - emails arrive in ProtonMail immediately
- Gmail keeps a copy in your inbox (if you chose that option)
- You can turn off forwarding anytime
- ProtonMail free tier gives you 1GB storage
- If you need more ProtonMail storage, consider upgrading
